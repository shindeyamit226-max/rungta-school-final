Admission Procedure

How toApply

Step 1: Registration

Obtain the Admission cum Registration Form from the school office on payment of Rs. 600/- or register online. Submit the completed form with required documents.

Step 2: Documentation

Submit birth certificate, previous school TC, mark sheets, Aadhaar card, photographs, and address proof as per the admission requirements.

Step 3: Interaction

As per the RTE Act, there will be no written test for classes Nursery to VIII. Admission is based on age criteria and informal interaction. For classes IX & XI, performance in previous exams is considered.

Step 4: Admission

All admissions are considered purely on merit. Upon selection, complete the fee payment and join the Rungta family!

Withdrawal & Refund
