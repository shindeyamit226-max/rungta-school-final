Introduction

School Board &Affiliations

CBSE Affiliation

Rungta Public School is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. The school follows the CBSE curriculum and prepares students for the All India Secondary School Examination (AISSE) and All India Senior School Certificate Examination (AISSCE).

Cambridge International

The school also offers Cambridge International Curriculum, providing students with globally recognized qualifications that open doors to universities worldwide.

General Instructions

  • School timings: 8:00 AM to 2:30 PM
  • Students must wear proper school uniform
  • Regular attendance is mandatory (minimum 75%)
  • Parent-teacher meetings are held periodically
  • Students must carry school ID card daily

Rules & Regulations

  • Respect for teachers, staff, and fellow students
  • No mobile phones allowed on campus
  • Bullying or ragging will result in strict disciplinary action
  • School property must be treated with care
  • Students must maintain discipline at all times

Discipline Codes

The school maintains a strict discipline code to ensure a safe and conducive learning environment. Students are expected to uphold the values of honesty, integrity, and respect.

Child Care and Protection Policy

Rungta Public School is committed to the safety and well-being of every child. We have a comprehensive child protection policy that ensures a safe, nurturing, and supportive environment for all students.
