Sports &Culture

Active involvement in Sports and Cultural activities is essential for all-round development. The school lays equal emphasis on Academics, Sports & Cultural Activities.

⚽

Games & Sports

Football, Cricket, Badminton, Basketball, Volleyball, Table Tennis, Athletics, Taekwondo, Horse Riding, Chess and a Toddler's pool.

🎭

Performing Arts

Music – Vocal and Instrumental, Dance – Indian and Western, Drawing, Painting, Block Printing, Embroidery, Needle Work and Craftwork.

🏆

Tournament & Meets

Children participate in various Tournaments and Cultural and Sports Meets at Intra and Inter School level.

🎪

Festivals & Celebrations

The school is secular in nature. All major festivals are enacted and celebrated to inculcate Unity in Diversity.

🚌

Field Trips & Excursions

Children are taken on field trips to various industries and places of educational interest.

🏠

House System

Four houses promoting healthy competition, leadership, and team spirit among students.

Community Service

Students actively participate in community service programs including environmental awareness drives, visits to old age homes, and social welfare activities.

Exchange Program

Cultural exchange programs that expose students to diverse perspectives and global cultures, broadening their horizons.
