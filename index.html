Who We Are

We Nurture Brilliance. We Inspire Excellence.

Our Legacy

Beginning humbly in 1999 with an intake of just 240 students, the G.D. Rungta Educational Society has experienced phenomenal growth. Today, our various institutions proudly educate more than 4,000 students from across the country.

Our Mission

We believe that when students are encouraged to explore their interests, dreams, and inner motivations, they create meaningful stories, ideas, and expressions that help them understand themselves better.

Our Values

  • Respect for all
  • Perseverance
  • Selfless Service
  • Creative Thinking
  • Confidence Building
